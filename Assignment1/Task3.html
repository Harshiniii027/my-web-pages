My Favorite Recipe: Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 large Onions (sliced)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Fresh Coriander & Mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or Ghee

Steps to Prepare

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil/ghee and fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add whole spices and ginger-garlic paste, sauté until aromatic.
  4. Add chicken pieces, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until chicken is sealed.
  5. Add tomatoes and yogurt, cook until the chicken is tender and gravy thickens.
  6. In a pot, layer half of the rice, then chicken masala, then remaining rice.
  7. Top with fried onions, mint, coriander, and garam masala. Drizzle some ghee.
  8. Cover tightly and cook on low flame (dum) for 20–25 minutes.
  9. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.

Cooking Terms

Dum
Slow cooking method by sealing the pot to trap steam and enhance flavor.
Marinate
Soaking food in a mixture (spices, yogurt) to enhance flavor and tenderness.
Tempering
Frying spices in hot oil or ghee to release their aroma and taste.
